Key Mathematical Formulas

Plant Density Formula

Number of Plants = Total Garden Area (m²) ÷ Space per Plant (m²)

This calculation helps maximize space utilization without overcrowding plants.

Water Requirement Formula

Water Needed (L) = Garden Area (m²) × Water per m² (L/day)

Essential for planning irrigation systems and water conservation.

Yield Estimation Formula

Yield (kg) = Number of Plants × Average Yield per Plant (kg)

Helps predict food production for household or community needs.
